## **Introduction**

TradeBrix are excited to announce the launch of our new Rewards Points System — built specifically for prop firms looking to increase conversions, improve retention, and scale more sustainably.

In today’s prop trading environment, acquisition costs are rising and competition is intensifying. Most firms compete on price, discounts, or profit splits. But long-term growth isn’t driven by flash sales, it’s driven by engagement.

That’s where rewards infrastructure changes the game.

## **Turning Transactions into Ecosystems**

Traditionally, the prop firm journey has been simple: a trader purchases a challenge, attempts to pass, and if they fail, they often leave. This creates a high-churn model where lifetime value remains limited and firms are forced to constantly replace lost users.

A structured rewards system shifts this dynamic.

Instead of a one-time transaction, traders begin accumulating value through actions: completing onboarding, organic interactions, passing phases, referring friends, or engaging in the community. Even if a trader fails a challenge, they don’t walk away empty-handed — they’ve earned progress.

That psychological shift is powerful. When traders feel they are building toward something, conversion rates increase and post-failure drop off rates decrease.

## **Increasing Conversion Without Discount Dependency**

Many firms rely heavily on 30–50% discount campaigns to drive sales. Firms looking to rethink their broader acquisition approach can explore more sustainable strategies in our detailed breakdown of prop firm marketing strategies.

While effective in the short term, this approach compresses margins and trains traders to wait for promotions.

A reward system replaces constant discounting with earned incentives.

Instead of offering blanket sales, firms can reward specific behaviors, such as engaging on socials, completing trading-related objectives, or progressing through evaluation stages with points redeemable for challenge discounts, free retries, or account upgrades. This not only protects margins but also reinforces positive trading behavior.

The result is higher-quality engagement and stronger brand positioning.

## **Retention Is the Real Growth Engine**

The most scalable prop firms aren’t simply those with the most traffic, they’re the ones that retain users longest.

Reward infrastructure increases retention by creating ongoing progression. Points, tiers, and unlockable benefits introduce gamification and visible advancement. Traders remain engaged because they see measurable progress within the ecosystem.

As retention improves, so does lifetime value. Traders are more likely to repurchase, upgrade accounts, and refer others. Over time, this compounds revenue without requiring proportional increases in marketing spend. We explore retention frameworks and trader lifecycle strategies more deeply in our guide to attracting and retaining high-quality traders.

## **Data-Driven Scaling**

Beyond engagement, reward systems generate valuable behavioral data. Firms can identify which actions correlate with repurchases, which users show high LTV characteristics, and which engagement patterns predict churn.

This insight allows for smarter segmentation, personalized offers, and automated retention campaigns all essential for scaling efficiently. For a deeper look at tracking lifetime value, acquisition cost, and full-funnel performance, see our analysis of maximizing marketing ROI for prop firms.

In short, reward infrastructure transforms growth from reactive to strategic.
